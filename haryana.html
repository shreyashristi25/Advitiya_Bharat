Haryana

The land of green fields, folk music, and vibrant fairs.

← Back to States

Festivals

Surajkund Mela is Haryana’s most famous crafts fair, attracting artisans from across India. Teej and Gugga Naumi are other major festivals.

Traditional Arts & Crafts

Haryana is known for Phulkari embroidery, pottery, and woodwork. The state’s crafts reflect its rural traditions and vibrant colors.

Folk Dances

Dhamal and Ghoomar are popular folk dances, performed during festivals and celebrations. Phag is another traditional dance of Haryana.

Handicrafts & Textiles

Haryana is famous for clay pottery, handloom weaving, and basketry. The state’s markets are filled with unique handmade items.

Unique Facts

Haryana is home to the Sultanpur Bird Sanctuary, the Kurukshetra battlefield of the Mahabharata, and is known as the “Milk Pail of India.”
