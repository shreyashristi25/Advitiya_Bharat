Tripura

The land of bamboo, tribal culture, and royal heritage.

Festivals

Kharchi is Tripura's most important festival, celebrated with traditional rituals. Garia and Ker Puja are other major festivals.

Traditional Arts & Crafts

Tripura is known for bamboo and cane crafts, handloom weaving, and wood carving. The state's artisans create beautiful baskets and textiles.

Folk Dances

Hojagiri and Garia are popular folk dances, performed during festivals and celebrations. Lebang Bumani is another traditional dance.

Handicrafts & Textiles

Tripura is famous for handloom textiles, bamboo products, and cane furniture. The state's markets are filled with unique handmade items.

Unique Facts

Tripura is home to the Ujjayanta Palace, Neermahal (water palace), and is known for its rich tribal culture and biodiversity.
