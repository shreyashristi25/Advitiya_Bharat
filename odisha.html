Odisha

The land of temples, classical dance, and vibrant festivals.

← Back to States

Festivals

Rath Yatra at Puri is Odisha’s grandest festival, celebrated with chariot processions. Durga Puja and Konark Dance Festival are other major events.

Traditional Arts & Crafts

Odisha is famous for Pattachitra painting, stone carving, and silver filigree. The state’s artisans are skilled in intricate artwork and sculpture.

Folk Dances

Odissi is a classical dance form, known for its grace and expressiveness. Chhau and Gotipua are other traditional dances.

Handicrafts & Textiles

Odisha is renowned for Sambalpuri sarees, Bomkai textiles, and applique work. The state’s markets are filled with unique handmade items.

Unique Facts

Odisha is home to the Konark Sun Temple (UNESCO site), Chilika Lake, and a rich tradition of classical dance and music.
