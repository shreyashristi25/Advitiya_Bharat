The land of Kuchipudi, vibrant festivals, and rich Telugu heritage.

Festivals

Ugadi marks the Telugu New Year and is celebrated with special dishes and rituals. Sankranti is a harvest festival with kite flying and traditional sweets. Tirupati Brahmotsavam is a grand temple festival attracting millions of devotees.

Traditional Arts & Crafts

Andhra Pradesh is famous for Kalamkari hand-painted textiles, Kondapalli toys made from softwood, and Etikoppaka lacquerware. These crafts reflect the region‚Äôs artistic legacy.

Folk Dances

Kuchipudi is the classical dance form of Andhra Pradesh, known for its grace and expressive storytelling. Other folk dances include Veeranatyam and Butta Bommalu.

Handicrafts & Textiles

The state is renowned for Mangalagiri sarees, Venkatagiri sarees, and Uppada silk. These textiles are prized for their quality and intricate designs.

Unique Facts

Andhra Pradesh is home to the world-famous Tirumala Venkateswara Temple in Tirupati, one of the richest and most visited temples globally. The state‚Äôs coastline is the second longest in India.
