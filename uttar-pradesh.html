Uttar Pradesh

The heart of India - land of the Taj Mahal, Kumbh Mela, and rich heritage.

Festivals

Kumbh Mela at Prayagraj is the world's largest religious gathering. Ram Navami and Krishna Janmashtami are other major festivals.

Traditional Arts & Crafts

Uttar Pradesh is famous for Chikan embroidery, Zardozi work, and Banarasi silk. The state's artisans are skilled in intricate textile work.

Folk Dances

Kathak is the classical dance form, while Raslila and Nautanki are popular folk performances.

Handicrafts & Textiles

Uttar Pradesh is renowned for Banarasi sarees, Chikan work, and brassware. The state's markets are filled with unique handmade items.

Unique Facts

Uttar Pradesh is home to the Taj Mahal (UNESCO site), Varanasi (the spiritual capital), and is India's most populous state.
