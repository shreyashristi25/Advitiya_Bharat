Uttarakhand

Dev Bhoomi - the land of gods, temples, and spiritual heritage.

Festivals

Kumbh Mela at Haridwar is one of the world's largest religious gatherings. Uttarayani and Phool Dei are other important festivals.

Traditional Arts & Crafts

Uttarakhand is known for wood carving, stone carving, and handloom weaving. The state's artisans create beautiful temple art and traditional textiles.

Folk Dances

Jhora and Chholiya are popular folk dances, performed during festivals and celebrations. Barada Nati is another traditional dance.

Handicrafts & Textiles

Uttarakhand is famous for handloom textiles, woolen shawls, and metal crafts. The state's markets are filled with unique handmade items.

Unique Facts

Uttarakhand is home to the Char Dham (four sacred temples), Valley of Flowers National Park, and is known as "Dev Bhoomi" (Land of Gods).
